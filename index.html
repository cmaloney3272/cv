Cassandra L. Maloney

Alexander Technique and Yoga Instructor


Cassie aspires to bring expressive movement to the lives of others through vinyasa yoga, slackline yoga, and other creative practices. Her classes, which are infused with mindfulness and meditation, are built to safely balance strength and softness. She sees her practice of teaching as art meant to inspire and create ease in the whole being. Cassie creates a lighthearted environment that encourages playfulness and explorative boundary-pushing for students of all levels and abilities.
